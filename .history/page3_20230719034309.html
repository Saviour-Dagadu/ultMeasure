Research Manager

Francis is a research specialist with over 10 years of experience. He has skills and competencies in data collection, processing and management, and project planning, monitoring, and evaluation.

Through professional assignments, he has developed capacity in qualitative and quantitative analysis, Database and Data collection App development, the building of data tools, facilitation of training, report writing, and communication skills with outstanding interpersonal abilities.

He has worked on several research/surveys and projects with several research organizations including Noguchi, ISSER, SPH, and ICI amongst others.

Francis has a degree in BSc. Industrial arts from College of Art.
